MY WORK SPEAKS ABOUT DUALISTIC FLOW OF LIFE

OPTICAL ART / ACRYLIC ON CANVAS

Identity / Narrative

Verónica Carrasquel is a self-taught artist from Caracas, Venezuela, currently based in Buenos Aires. Her work explores the duality of life through the precision of optical art, using acrylic on canvas.

Current Location

Buenos Aires, Argentina

Medium

Acrylic on Canvas

The Rhythm of Precision

Influenced by the kinetic movement and mid-century aesthetic, every stroke is a calculated meditation on balance. The black and white palette serves as the purest expression of contrast and unity.
